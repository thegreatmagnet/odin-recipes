Clams with white wine

Description

Clams steamed with white wine and red chili flakes

Ingredients

  • Clams
  • White wine
  • Red chili flakes
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Wash clams
  2. Heat olive oil and cook garlic
  3. Add clams and wine
  4. Add red chili flakes
  5. Cover pan and steam 5-6 minutes

Michelada

Description

Beer cocktail with Clamato and chili lime salt and hot sauce

This is delicious and anyone who thinks Clamato is disgusting has no taste

Ingredients

  • Beer
  • Clamato
  • Chili lime salt
  • Maggi seasoning
  • Mexican hot sauce

Steps

  1. Pour beer in glass
  2. Add Clamato and seasonsings
  3. Stir
  4. Put on some ranchero music and enjoy
